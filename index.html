Geometric Facility Layout

A 3D visualization of how geometry and mathematical concepts create efficient production facilities

Project Overview

This project demonstrates how geometric principles influence factory design. By visualizing layouts in 3D, we can analyze efficiency, symmetry, and spatial optimization ‚Äî bridging mathematics and engineering concepts in a tangible, interactive way.

Mathematical Concepts Applied

Symmetry

Ensures balanced and aesthetically pleasing arrangements in the facility.

Area & Volume

Used to estimate space occupied by machines and pathways to avoid congestion.

Angles & Pathways

Helps determine optimal flow and movement directions inside the layout.
